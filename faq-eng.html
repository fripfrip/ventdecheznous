FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the difference between a small wind turbine and a commercial wind turbine ?

The purpose of a small wind turbine is for commercial and business use, whereas commercial wind turbines are typically used in industrial parks. In addition, small wind turbines are significantly lighter than their traditional counterparts, they operate at only 2m/sec wind gusts compared to 6 m/sec wind gusts for industrial wind turbines, produce minimal noise, and are built using modern innovative technologies.

Is the Hummer Generator Invisible Wind Turbine noisy ?

Quite the opposite – the Hummer Generator Invisible Wind Turbine is over 10 per cent less noisy than traditional small wind turbines. You can carry a full conversation below the wind turbine while it is operating at full force.

How long does it take to set up and install the Hummer Generator Wind Turbine ?

The manufacturing, shipping and installation process for the Hummer Generator Invisible Wind Turbine takes up to three months.

Are there any seasonal installation conditions ?

The Hummer Generator Invisible Wind Turbine can be installed at any time of the year, as long as the field is clear and not flooded.

Am I allowed to install a Hummer Generator Invisible Wind Turbine on my property ?

Everyone has the right to install a wind turbine on their property, however some municipalities do have some rules regarding wind turbine height and distance from other property. Please contact your local municipality for specific regulations.
