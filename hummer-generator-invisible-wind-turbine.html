WIND TURBINE

General Characteristics

The Hummer Generator Invisible Wind Turbine can be recognized for its original design due to its integration of the SCF Supercritical Generator Technology, an award-winning generator. While other wind turbines often place their generator in the yaw shaft, the Hummer’s generator is strategically placed in the nose cone. Due to this structural design, the Hummer Generator Invisible Wind Turbine can save between 10 and 30 per cent more energy than a traditional wind turbine. As a result, kinetic energy is transmitted directly to the generator rather than passing through the yaw shaft, and creating mechanical energy more efficiently.

With many high-tech attributes, consumers can expect to operate a wind turbine that is:

  • Lighter
  • Smaller
  • More efficient
  • Less noisy
  • Distinct in appearance
  • Easier to assemble

Other features

  • Small wind turbine: conceived for business use and commercial use
  • Converts wind into energy for reinsertion into the battery or the electricity network
  • New generation of high-tech small wind turbines: operates at wind gusts of 2m/sec compared to traditional domestic wind turbines, which operate at wind gusts of 4m/sec
  • Patented Technology: holding seven patents both nationally and internationally, the SCF Supercritical Generator won first prize at the 37th annual Brussels International Eureka Contest

Model compartments

  • Blades
  • Hydraulic Brake : Brake block assembled in the flange of wind generator for hydraulic breaking
  • Generator: Extremely light and small, low in sound, swift at start-up, quick in heat dissipation, high in efficiency (>0.8)
  • Siemens PLC Controller: Receives wind signal and operation signal, reacts quickly, intelligent controllers, also includes digital touch screen for auto/manual control
  • Off-Grid Inverter: Adopts SPWM technology, an amorphous transformer and high frequency breaker; high conversion efficiency, stable frequency output, stable voltage, noise filtration, and low distortion of pure sine waves
  • Grid-Tied Inverter: Adopts MPPT and IGBT technologies, with high power generating capacity, and wide AC voltage range
  • Dumping Load: Metal dumping box, dissipates heat by air convection, without the fan and the controlling box
  • Rectifier: Rectifies and controls the dumping load, sends the rectifier send all normal electrical load to the inverter whereas the power surge is sent to the dumping load.
  • Yaw Shaft: Home to the power supply and gear box, controlling the operation of wind generator
  • Dogvane and Anemometer: Indicates wind direction and measures wind speed, transmitting wind signal to PLC controller.
