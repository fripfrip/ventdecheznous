ABOUT US

Incorporated since 2003 and working in the field of dual energy alongside with HydroQuebec, the company changed their brand name in 2001, in order to better reflect their new mandate - renewable energy.

VENT DE CHEZ NOUS holds exclusive distribution rights for the Hummer Generator Invisible Wind Turbine in Canada and in the United States.

Our goal is to provide self-financing wind turbines as a result of their energy savings. We are proud to distribute high-tech, top quality products built in optimal manufacturing conditions for quality assurance.
